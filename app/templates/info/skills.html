Info: skills

To be able to reserve and use a machine, you need to necessary skill.
A skill can be assigned to your account by a moderator.
You can browse the available skills here, and you can see your assigned skills on your profile page.

To get a skill, you can follow a initiation course. Please contact info@budalab.be for more details.

If you believe you don't need an initiation, please contact us at info@budalab.be.
